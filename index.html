LITHO
PRINT.

Sherbet Ink loves paper and all the juicy, clever bits that go into producing beautiful and ethical litho print, I suppose you could say we live, eat and breathe it. Let’s face it, print isn’t rocket science but with the right approach it can be clever, simple, effective, gorgeous to look at and bespoke. We specialise in being different and thinking differently about our print. We are eco printing specialists and all our work is produced with the environment in mind.

In a nutshell, we care about your print and how it is produced.

Sherbet Ink is dedicated to bespoke eco print and understands that producing gorgeous print to a boutique finish is a fine art. We believe paper choice and print ethics are an essential core element to your design, brand and image. veggie ink, FSC, peFC, ISO9001, ISO14001, carbon neutral or chem free may sound like gobbledegook to you, but it’s sweet music to our ears. We are eco print crazy and proud of it.

We care about you, your company and your brand. We can help you get all the crucial marketing elements right, whilst sav- ing you time and money. We have amazingly competitive pricing, beautiful FSC certified paper as standard, and fantastic relationships with our dedicated and trusted local printers, printing from 1 to 10 colours in a single pass. We also specialise in all your print finishing needs like folding, bindery, die cutting, surface effects, spot colours, metallics and all kinds of other print related stuff. Save money and feel all warm and fluffy about your print.

Sherbet Ink can help protect and improve the environmental image of your business, save you money and make you feel happy with your print. If you are interested in changing the way you think about your print then get in touch. We would love the opportunity to have a chat about your next project.

DIGITAL
PRINT.

Digital print really has come a long way in quality, not quite up to Litho standards but if you are looking for small quantities or customised/ personalised print then this is pretty cool stuff. We link the latest digi-press technology with quick turnaround times and database mail sortation systems and hey-presto you have a powerful on-demand print service. variable data is certainly an area of print that is on-the-up.

Linked to targeted mail sortation and marketing services you can benefit from a bespoke product with lower costs all round. Digital is a high quality, low cost solution which is quick and efficient. What more could you ask for! Whether you need a bunch of business cards, a short-run booklet or a database marketing cam- paign, we can help. oh, and of course we obviously print it on FSC paper.
